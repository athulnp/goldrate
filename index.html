Gold Rate Alert System
To send SMS alerts, this app uses Twilio — a free SMS service. You'll need to set up a free account.
  • 1Sign up at twilio.com/try-twilio (free trial includes ~$15 credit)
  • 2From the Console Dashboard, copy your Account SID and Auth Token
  • 3Get a free Twilio phone number from Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a number
  • 4Enter your credentials in the panel → click Save & Start Monitoring
Gold prices are fetched from the free metals-api.com or gold-api.com. Your credentials are stored only in your browser's localStorage — never sent anywhere except Twilio.
